\subsection{Detailed Description}
\hyperlink{classDoubleSolenoid}{DoubleSolenoid} class for running 2 channels of high voltage Digital Output (9472 module).

The \hyperlink{classDoubleSolenoid}{DoubleSolenoid} class is typically used for pneumatics solenoids that have two positions controlled by two separate channels.
