\subsection{\-Detailed \-Description}
\hyperlink{classDoubleSolenoid}{\-Double\-Solenoid} class for running 2 channels of high voltage \-Digital \-Output (9472 module).

\-The \hyperlink{classDoubleSolenoid}{\-Double\-Solenoid} class is typically used for pneumatics solenoids that have two positions controlled by two separate channels.
